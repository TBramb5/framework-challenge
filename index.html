Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Jurassic Park Located?

Jurassic Park is located on Isla Nublar, a secluded island approximately 120 miles west of Costa Rica

Are the Dinosaurs Real?

Yes, all dinosaurs in the park are living, breathing animals created through genetic engineering

Is it Safe to Visit Jurassic Park?

Absolutely! All dinosaurs are contained within carefully engineered paddocks, and guests are never placed in unsafe areas

What if the Power Goes Out?

Our chief systems architect, Dennis Nedry, has built a command network to ensure smooth park operations even in the event of a power outage

Can Visitors Stay at the Park?

Guests will stay at the luxurious Jurassic Park Resort, located near the main Visitor Center on Isla Nublar

What Can Visitors do at the Park?

Visitors can enjoy guided tours, educational exhibits, and feeding demonstrations. Jeep safaris offer safe, close-up dinosaur viewing

What Kind of Dinosaurs are at Jurassic Park?

Jurassic Park features iconic dinosaurs like T-Rex, Velociraptor, Brachiosaurus, and Triceratops. With more dinosaur species coming soon!
