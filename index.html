Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is Jurassic Park?

Jurassic Park is a fictional dinosaur theme park.

Q2: When was the park supposed to open?

The park was scheduled to open in Summer 1993.

Q3: Where is the park located?

On Isla Nublar, a fictional island near Costa Rica.

Q4: Are the dinosaurs real?

In the story, yes, cloned from ancient DNA.

Q5: Can I buy tickets now?

The park never officially opened, so tickets are not available.

Q6: What safety measures are in place?

The park uses high-tech containment systems.

Q7: Who created Jurassic Park?

John Hammond, the founder of InGen, envisioned and built the park.

Q8: What kinds of dinosaurs can visitors expect?

The park features species like T-Rex, Velociraptors, Triceratops, and Brachiosaurus.
